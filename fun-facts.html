Professional E-Sports

I was also a professional E-sport player for a while. In 2017, during a difficult period in my life, I spent most of my time playing Pro Evolution Soccer 2017. I became so good at it that I was ranked 45th in the American continent. Konami even flew me to LA to compete in the US National Championship (with food and hotel all paid), but I sadly lost the semi-final by one goal, so I didn't get to play in the continental final.
I still play PES from time to time, staying around the top 1000 in the world, though I'm not as good as I used to be. You can check out some of my recent gameplay on my YouTube channel!
